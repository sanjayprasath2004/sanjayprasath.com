About Me

I am a postgraduate student pursuing a Master of Social Work at VIT Vellore, supported by a solid academic background in computer applications. My academic and fieldwork experiences have strengthened my competencies in community engagement, organisational functioning, programme implementation, and evidence-based social interventions. I am committed to advancing inclusive growth, strengthening welfare mechanisms, and integrating both social work principles and technological insights to support effective and meaningful social change.
